My Golf Tips

                            
                
                        
  • Master the Fundamentals: Focus on grip, stance, and alignment.
    •                     
  • Focus on Tempo: Maintain a controlled swing speed rather than swinging as hard as possible.
    •                     
  • Practice Smart: Work on your short game, more than your full swing—it saves strokes.
    •                     
  • Learn Course Management: Avoid unnecessary risks.
    •                     
  • Enjoy the Game: It's just you in nature. Understand it takes patience and practice.
